1. text-wrap: balance

Text wrapping is an essential CSS feature that determines how text content flows within an element.
By default, text wraps at the edge of a container, often leading to unbalanced lines where one line could have many words, and another only one or two.

The text-wrap: balance property, introduced recently, offers a solution to uneven wrapping by attempting to balance the text more evenly across multiple lines.
